Procurement Team

Procurement Contracting Officer (PCO), Contracting Officer's Representative (COR), and Accessibility Subject Matter Expert (SME} all play a critical role in choosing digital tools that meet the organization's needs.

{% asset circle-124.png class="usa-media-block__img" alt="Alt text" %}

Agency Project Lead / Requiring Official

The project lead plays a critical role in the project, including it's accessibility. This often starts with thinking about inclusion in the discovery and market research phases.

{% asset circle-124.png class="usa-media-block__img" alt="Alt text" %}

Vendors

Vendors want to demonstrate that their software best meets the needs of the clients. Accessibility is a critical part of many organizations requirements, and so it must be considred.

{% asset circle-124.png class="usa-media-block__img" alt="Alt text" %}

Accessibility Professionals and ACR Authors

It is a good practice for a vendor to engage with a 3rd party to create the ACR. It is difficult for many vendors to provide an objective view of how closely their software aligns with accessibility requirements.
